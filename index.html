Welcome to the official website of Lilac Jam, a rockin' band from South Dakota. Our alternative rock, punk, and grunge influences have inspired us to create some rockin' tunes I think you'll love, and we can't wait to share them with you.

On our website, you can check out our latest tracks, watch videos of our electrifying live performances, and stay up to date on all of our upcoming shows. You can also get to know the members of the band and learn more about our influences and inspirations.

We are constantly putting our whole heart into our music, and we're always experimenting with new sounds and styles.

Make sure to follow us on social media to stay in the loop and be the first to hear about new music, shows, and more. We can't wait to connect with you and share our passion for music.

Thanks for visiting our website, and we hope to see you at one of our shows soon!
